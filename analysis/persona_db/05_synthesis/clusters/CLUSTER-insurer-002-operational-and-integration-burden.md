# Cluster: Operational + Integration Burden

## Definition
Adoption is constrained by interoperability and integration feasibility with the national backbone (EDS/FSE interaction) and by the work required to meet outcome measurement and privacy compliance expectations. (Claims: CLAIM-20260225-014, CLAIM-20260225-015)

## Separating Variables
- Integration feasibility across Regions vs local-only feasibility (Claim: CLAIM-20260225-014)
- Measurement and privacy compliance overhead vs speed-to-pilot pressure (Claim: CLAIM-20260225-015)

## Persona Members
- Pilot-Forward Product Owner (ID: PERSONA-insurer-003-pilot-forward-product-owner)
- Workflow-Protecting Ops Manager (ID: PERSONA-insurer-004-workflow-protecting-ops-manager)

## Shared Claims
- Interoperability constraints are set by the national EDS and its interaction with FSE and national systems. (ID: CLAIM-20260225-014)
- Outcome measurement + privacy-compliant health-data utilization criteria (especially for AI/DTx). (ID: CLAIM-20260225-015)
- Procurement/HTA appraisal processes and recommendations support policy and procurement decisions. (ID: CLAIM-20260225-013)
