# Cluster: Legitimacy + Access Friction

## Definition
Extended diagnostic uncertainty (including repeated consultations and conflicting opinions) is compounded by barriers like stigma, logistics, "normal" results, and affordability pressures, leading to legitimacy-seeking language and a desire for clinician-ready artifacts that reduce delay. (Claim: CLAIM-20260224-009)

## Separating Variables
- Degree of access friction (logistics/affordability) vs capacity to persist (Claim: CLAIM-20260224-009)
- Need for legitimacy and being believed (Claim: CLAIM-20260224-010)
