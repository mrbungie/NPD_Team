# Claim: Patients need a clinician-usable summary and appointment-prep scaffolding

## Statement
Patients struggle to translate messy symptom notes into something a clinician can use quickly, and value a reusable one-page summary plus a visit-prep checklist (what to bring/ask).

## Evidence Basis
- "I track dates and triggers because otherwise I forget. The hardest part is translating it into something the specialist can use in 10 minutes." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-016)
- "A checklist for what to bring and what to ask, plus a reusable summary so the clinician starts at page 2, not page 1." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-016)

## Reasoning
This is a concrete unmet need that maps directly to product outputs (structured summary + prep prompts) and reduces repeated retelling.
