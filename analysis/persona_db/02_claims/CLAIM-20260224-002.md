# Claim: Patients use online communities to manage uncertainty

## Statement
When causes and treatments are uncertain, patients use online communities to seek information and social support.

## Evidence Basis
- Reddit long-hauler analysis highlights information-seeking ("This is happening to me") and support-seeking ("Is this happening to you?") functions. (Source: SOURCE-20260224-002)

## Reasoning
This is observed behavior from large-scale social media data and maps directly to DO/SAY for uncertain symptom experiences.
