# Claim: Purchaser operational capacity is a constraint

## Statement
Employer benefits teams often have limited capacity to negotiate and adjudicate complex performance-based contracts.

## Evidence Basis
- PHTI PBC notes executing these contracts remains challenging, particularly for employer benefits teams with limited capacity. (Source: SOURCE-20260224-009)

## Reasoning
This becomes a product requirement: reduce operational burden, simplify measurement/reconciliation.
