# Claim: Bridge community insights into a clinician-acceptable narrative

## Statement
Patients want a bridge from community sensemaking to clinician acceptance: compare symptoms to real patterns without judgement, then generate a script/one-page summary so they are not improvising under stress.

## Evidence Basis
- "I need a way to compare my symptoms to real patterns without being judged, and then a way to turn it into something a doctor will accept." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-017)
- "A script for what to say, plus a one-page summary so I'm not improvising while stressed." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-017)
- "A single place where my story is saved, plus a calmer explanation of what the results mean." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-018)

## Reasoning
This combines do/say/want with a journey breakdown fix: reduce cognitive load and improve clinician handoff.
