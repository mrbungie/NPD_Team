# Claim: Evidence-driven patients build a clinician-ready symptom summary, then triangulate with peers

## Statement
Evidence-driven patients use search and symptom checkers to produce a structured symptom list/timeline for their MMG, then triangulate with peer stories to look for pattern clues and next tests.

## Evidence Basis
- "I searched first, then used a symptom checker because I wanted a structured list to bring to my MMG. After that I posted in a forum because I wanted to know if anyone had the same pattern and what tests they did." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-015)

## Reasoning
This describes a product-relevant early-journey behavior: structured self-triage is used as preparation for a clinician conversation, not just for reassurance.
