# Claim: Legitimacy fatigue drives a shift from searching to appointment prep

## Statement
Legitimacy-fatigued patients reduce tool/search usage because they want less noise; they shift effort toward appointment preparation and finding clinicians who will not dismiss them.

## Evidence Basis
- "I stopped using checkers because I don't want more noise. I focus on preparing for appointments because I only get a few minutes and I can't waste them." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-021)
- "At this point I'm not searching for diagnoses, I'm searching for a clinician who won't dismiss me." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-022)

## Reasoning
This is a product insight: "more information" can be negative value; success is appointment readiness and legitimacy support.
