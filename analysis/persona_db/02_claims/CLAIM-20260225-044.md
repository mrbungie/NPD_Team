# Claim: Workflow protection is the ops manager's core job

## Statement
Operations managers see their primary job as protecting workflows; solutions that introduce manual work are expected to fail adoption.

## Evidence Basis
- "My job is to protect workflows. If the tool creates manual work, adoption fails." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-029)

## Reasoning
This sets a product constraint: zero- or low-manual-work integration and clear workflow impact mapping.
