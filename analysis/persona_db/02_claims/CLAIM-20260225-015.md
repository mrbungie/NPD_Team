# Claim: Italy evaluation for AI/DTx centers outcome measurement and privacy compliance

## Statement
In Italy, AGENAS is developing an HTA evaluation framework for AI systems and Digital Therapeutics (DTx), including criteria for outcome measurement and for health-data utilization compliant with privacy regulations.

## Evidence Basis
- AGENAS explicitly describes the development of an HTA evaluation framework for AI/DTx and criteria for health-data utilization compliant with privacy regulations. (Source: SOURCE-20260225-007)

## Reasoning
This replaces US purchaser language ("outcomes, not engagement") with an Italy-specific evaluation anchor that still drives measurement and auditability requirements.
