# Claim: Symptom checkers can increase anxiety for some users

## Statement
Symptom checker app use may unsettle health-anxious users due to risk-averse triage and unlikely but serious diagnosis suggestions.

## Evidence Basis
- The paper notes users with high health anxiety may be further unsettled by risk-averse triage and unlikely serious diagnosis suggestions. (Source: SOURCE-20260224-003)

## Reasoning
This supports safety and reassurance requirements and highlights risks of over-alarming UX.
