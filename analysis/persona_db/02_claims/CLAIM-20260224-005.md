# Claim: Symptom checker usage triggers varied behavioral reactions

## Statement
After using symptom checker apps, users may seek additional information, self-treat, wait-and-see, or contact healthcare; some choose not to discuss app use with clinicians.

## Evidence Basis
- Qualitative results describe a "broad spectrum of behavioral reactions" including internet searching, self-treatment, and healthcare contact; some explicitly did not plan to discuss SCA use with a doctor. (Source: SOURCE-20260224-004)

## Reasoning
This provides actionable DO detail for designing features around follow-through and clinician handoffs.
