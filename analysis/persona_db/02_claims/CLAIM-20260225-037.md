# Claim: Undefined outcomes/governance upfront is an immediate trust failure

## Statement
Insurer stakeholders treat proposals as untrustworthy when outcome measures and data governance are not defined upfront; "we'll figure it out after deployment" is a rejection trigger.

## Evidence Basis
- "Untrustworthy is when outcomes are vague and the data governance is 'we'll figure it out'." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-025)
- "Untrustworthy is when the vendor cannot define outcome measures and data governance upfront." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-026)

## Reasoning
This creates a strong precondition for pilots: measurement definitions and governance roles must exist before procurement momentum builds.
