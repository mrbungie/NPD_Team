# Claim: Night-time self-triage loops amplify anxiety

## Statement
Anxiety-prone patients can get trapped in a night-time self-triage loop where symptom checkers and searching amplify worst-case thinking and disrupt sleep/functioning.

## Evidence Basis
- "I used a checker at 2am and it made everything worse. Then I couldn't sleep." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-019)
- "Breakdown is the night-time loop. I google, I check, I panic, then I can't function the next day." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-019)
- "My brain goes to worst case, then I feel ashamed about it." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-020)

## Reasoning
This is a journey breakdown moment with direct design implications for tone, pacing, and escalation scaffolding.
