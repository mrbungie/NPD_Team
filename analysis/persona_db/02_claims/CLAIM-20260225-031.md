# Claim: Reduce retelling and provide owned next steps + escalation path

## Statement
Patients want to reduce the emotional and logistical cost of care by reusing a clinician-ready story, receiving a written next-step plan, and having an escalation path that does not require starting over; breakdowns occur when no one owns the next step.

## Evidence Basis
- "Something that reduces the re-telling and gives me a clinician-ready story that can't be dismissed as 'vague'." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-021)
- "Breakdown is when I leave with 'come back if it gets worse' and no one owns the next step." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-021)
- "A structured plan and a path to the next clinician that doesn't make me start from nothing." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-022)

## Reasoning
This combines a journey-map breakdown with concrete product outputs: reusable summary, written plan, and escalation routing.
