# Claim: Adoption must be defensible (clinical + economic) with monitoring

## Statement
Governance executives require adoption decisions to be defensible to auditors and citizens, typically demanding measurable clinical and economic outcomes plus an evidence-backed monitoring plan.

## Evidence Basis
- "We need defensible adoption: it must be measurable and explainable to auditors and citizens." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-025)
- "We need measurable pathway impact and governance, otherwise it's just noise." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-026)

## Reasoning
This frames procurement as a public-accountability exercise and translates into requirements for measurement, reporting, and narrative coherence.
