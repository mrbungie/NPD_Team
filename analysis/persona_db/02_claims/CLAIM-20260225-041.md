# Claim: Manual work and training burden are common rollout killers

## Statement
Operational feasibility often fails due to manual reconciliation, training burden, and unreliable data flow; pilots and rollouts die when they add manual steps or underestimate operational work.

## Evidence Basis
- "Untrustworthy is when the pilot looks good but the integration story is local-only and cannot scale." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-027)
- "My problem is rollout: pilots die when they add manual steps." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-028)
- "If it creates manual reconciliation, it won't survive." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-028)

## Reasoning
This DO pattern defines non-negotiable product constraints: minimize manual steps and be honest about training/ops workload.
