# Claim: Performance-based contracting is common and growing

## Statement
Many purchasers use or plan to use performance-based contracts tying payment to outcomes (clinical, engagement, or financial savings).

## Evidence Basis
- PHTI survey: nearly half using performance-based contracts; majority plan to use. (Source: SOURCE-20260224-008)
- PHTI playbook: defines PBC and its role in tying payments to outcomes. (Source: SOURCE-20260224-009)

## Reasoning
This shapes VALUE: vendors must be able to measure, share, and contract on outcomes.
