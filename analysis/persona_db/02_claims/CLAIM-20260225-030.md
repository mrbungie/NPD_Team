# Claim: Legitimacy fatigue produces performative coping and numbness

## Statement
Legitimacy fatigue creates an emotional toll where patients feel tired of proving they are not lying and feel they must "perform" to be listened to; some become numb and frame the experience as survival.

## Evidence Basis
- "I am tired of proving I'm not lying. I need a plan." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-021)
- "I think: I have to perform. If I cry, they stop listening. If I'm calm, they think it's not serious." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-021)
- "I'm numb now. It's survival." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-022)

## Reasoning
These empathy-map signals highlight the need for legitimacy-supporting structure and low-friction escalation without repeated proof.
