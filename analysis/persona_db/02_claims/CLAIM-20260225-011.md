# Claim: Italy record access is structured around FSE 2.0

## Statement
In Italy, FSE 2.0 is the single access point where a citizen can consult their digital health documents/data for SSN-provided services (and, since 2020, also private providers), independent of their Region.

## Evidence Basis
- Ministero della Salute explicitly describes FSE as a single access point and states cross-region access to SSN services. (Source: SOURCE-20260225-011)

## Reasoning
This grounds product assumptions about "where records live" and what interoperability and access expectations look like in Italy/SSN.
