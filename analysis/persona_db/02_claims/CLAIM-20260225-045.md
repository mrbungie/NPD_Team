# Claim: Manual reconciliation and inadequate testing capacity are common breakdown points

## Statement
Insurer operations stakeholders distrust vendors who promise results but later require manual reconciliation; proposals stall when integration touches clinical systems and there is insufficient time or capacity for testing.

## Evidence Basis
- "Untrustworthy is when they promise the moon and then require manual reconciliation." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-029)
- "Stall is when integration touches clinical systems and no one has time for testing." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-029)
- "Breakdown... we discover manual steps and it gets abandoned." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-029)

## Reasoning
This turns procurement into an execution-risk assessment: testing realism and workflow impact must be explicit.
