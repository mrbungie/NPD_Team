# Claim: Outcome ownership + monitoring credibility are common bottlenecks

## Statement
Procurement and scale-out often stall when no one can commit to outcome measurement ownership, data access, and a credible monitoring plan; manual reporting burdens are a negative outcome.

## Evidence Basis
- "We stall at the point where no one can commit to outcome measurement and data access." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-025)
- "We stall when the monitoring plan isn't credible and outcomes aren't defined." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-026)
- "Success metrics... ability to report it without manual work." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-025)

## Reasoning
This DO pattern highlights the need to pre-assign ownership and ship monitoring/reporting as a first-class deliverable.
