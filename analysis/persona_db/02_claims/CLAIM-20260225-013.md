# Claim: Italy procurement is mediated by national HTA processes (PNHTA-DM)

## Statement
In Italy, procurement/governance stakeholders use national HTA appraisal processes and recommendations (PNHTA-DM) to support policy and procurement decisions for medical devices, including digital health technologies.

## Evidence Basis
- AGENAS states PNHTA-DM includes appraisal processes and recommendations to support policy and procurement decisions. (Source: SOURCE-20260225-006)

## Reasoning
This replaces US "payer purchasing playbooks" with an Italy/SSN governance and procurement anchor.
