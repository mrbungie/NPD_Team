# Claim: Clear next-step ownership + reusable summary reduces momentum loss

## Statement
Patients want a clinician-ready symptom story plus a clear next-step fork that fits SSN reality; the journey breaks when tests are "normal" or when MMG-to-specialist handoffs have no clear owner.

## Evidence Basis
- "A short, clinician-ready summary with my timeline, what I've tried, and what changed week to week. And a sane 'next step' suggestion that fits the SSN reality." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-015)
- "The breakdown is after a 'normal' test: I leave without a next step. Then weeks pass, I spiral online, and I lose momentum booking the next appointment." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-015)
- "Breakdown is the handoff between MMG and specialist: I don't know who owns the next step, so I wait until things get worse." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-016)

## Reasoning
This captures a journey-map breakdown moment and a concrete "30% easier" opportunity: decision forks, ownership clarity, and reusable summaries.
