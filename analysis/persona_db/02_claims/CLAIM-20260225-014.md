# Claim: Italy EDS is a national interoperability/data backbone

## Statement
Italy established the Ecosistema dei Dati Sanitari (EDS) by Ministero della Salute decree (31 Dec 2024) as a centralized health data bank that interoperates with FSE and national systems such as Sistema TS.

## Evidence Basis
- AGENAS describes EDS establishment by decree and its role as a centralized data bank interacting with FSE and Sistema TS. (Source: SOURCE-20260225-013)

## Reasoning
This localizes "interoperability requirements" to Italy's actual national data backbone rather than US payer rules.
