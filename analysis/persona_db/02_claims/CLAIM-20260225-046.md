# Claim: DPIA/legal basis and governance clarity determine go/no-go

## Statement
Privacy/compliance risk is a frequent stop condition; procurement stalls on DPIA/legal basis and on unanswered governance questions (controller, access, consent), making auditability and minimization non-negotiable.

## Evidence Basis
- "Stall is DPIA and legal basis: if unclear, it stops." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-030)
- "Untrustworthy is when the vendor can't answer 'who is controller', 'who accesses data', and 'how is consent handled'." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-030)
- "Non-negotiable: privacy compliance, audit logs, and predictable workflow impact." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-029)

## Reasoning
This is a concrete privacy/governance checklist that must be addressed with documents and product features (logs, controls) before pilots.
