# Claim: AI in insurance has upside and harm modes

## Statement
AI tools in insurance can reduce administrative burden and delays by automating clearly approvable requests, but can also reinforce historically unjust denial patterns.

## Evidence Basis
- Stanford HAI key takeaways describe both benefits and risks, including reinforcing unjust denial patterns. (Source: SOURCE-20260224-010)

## Reasoning
This should shape product requirements for monitoring and appeal-support, not just automation.
