# Claim: Interoperability + DPIA clarity are common stall points

## Statement
Adoption stalls when solutions cannot explain or demonstrate interoperability with the national backbone (EDS/FSE) and when privacy impact assessment inputs are unclear; for technical stakeholders, integration feasibility is the product.

## Evidence Basis
- "We stall when the proposal is not interoperable with the national backbone or when privacy impact assessments are unclear." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-023)
- "Integration is the project. If integration fails, the tool doesn't exist." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-024)
- "Untrustworthy is when they say 'we integrate with FSE' but can't explain EDS/FSE data flows and security controls." (Source: SOURCE-20260225-024)

## Reasoning
This is DO behavior (how decisions stall) and a product packaging requirement: data flows, standards alignment, and testability must be explicit.
