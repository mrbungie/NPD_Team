# Source: "None of us are lying" (Brehon et al., 2023)

## Summary
56 qualitative interviews about needs, access barriers, and service quality for Long COVID; captures legitimacy-seeking dynamics.

## Evidence Snippet
> "When navigating Long COVID services... participants encountered barriers to access around stigma; appointment logistics; testing and 'normal' results; and financial precarity and affordability of services."

## Usage Notes
- Use for SAY: legitimacy seeking, stigma, and the need to be believed.
- Use for DO: self-advocacy and navigation burden.
