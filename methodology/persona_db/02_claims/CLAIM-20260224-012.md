# Claim: Low adoption of portal aggregation apps

## Statement
Only a small minority report using an app to combine information across different portals (7% in 2024).

## Evidence Basis
- Highlight: "only 7% reported using a portal organizing app". (Source: SOURCE-20260224-007)

## Reasoning
This indicates a gap between need (multi-portal) and current behavior (low consolidation), implying usability/trust or awareness barriers.
