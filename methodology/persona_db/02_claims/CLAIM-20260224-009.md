# Claim: Persistent-symptom journeys face stigma and logistics barriers

## Statement
Patients navigating persistent symptoms encounter access barriers including stigma, appointment logistics, normal test results, and affordability pressures.

## Evidence Basis
- Long COVID service navigation barriers include stigma; appointment logistics; testing and "normal" results; and financial precarity/affordability. (Source: SOURCE-20260224-005)

## Reasoning
This is direct evidence for the friction map and supports designing for admin burden reduction.
