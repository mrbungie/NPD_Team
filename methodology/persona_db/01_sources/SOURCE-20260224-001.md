# Source: Parents' Experiences of Navigating Diagnostic Uncertainty in Children With Rare Diseases (medRxiv, 2026)

## Summary
Qualitative narrative inquiry about diagnostic uncertainty and the pre-diagnosis period for rare diseases. Useful as evidence for "diagnostic odyssey" dynamics and legitimacy/"name" seeking.

## Evidence Snippet
> "For families, the period before diagnosis is often protracted and distressing, marked by repeated consultations, inconclusive investigations, conflicting medical opinions, and the absence of a recognisable name for their child's condition."

## Usage Notes
- Treat as direct evidence for "lack of a name" and repeated consultations during diagnostic uncertainty.
- Do not overgeneralize specifics (setting: Brazil, pediatric rare diseases) beyond what is stated.
