# Source: Unveiling Usage Patterns and Explaining Usage of Symptom Checker Apps (JMIR, 2024)

## Summary
48 symptom-checker users documented symptoms and app use over 6 weeks; mixed methods results include determinants, reactions, and unmet requirements.

## Evidence Snippet
> "The emergence of new and unfamiliar symptoms was a strong determinant for SCA use."

## Usage Notes
- Use for DO: symptom checker used at first symptom occurrence; users sometimes seek further info and self-treat.
- Use for VALUE: unmet needs include lack of personalization and nuanced symptom capture.
