# Cluster: Uncertainty Management

## Definition
Patients manage uncertainty by combining self-triage (symptom checkers) with peer validation and information-seeking in online communities, especially when symptoms are new or unfamiliar and anxiety can be amplified by alarming outputs. (Claim: CLAIM-20260224-004)

## Separating Variables
- Anxiety amplification vs reassurance-seeking behavior (Claim: CLAIM-20260224-008)
- Reliance on peer similarity seeking vs tool-driven navigation (Claim: CLAIM-20260224-002)

## Persona Members
- Evidence-Driven Navigator (ID: PERSONA-patient_unknown_symptom-001-evidence-driven-navigator)
- Community-Dependent Seeker (ID: PERSONA-patient_unknown_symptom-002-community-dependent-seeker)
- Anxious Reassurance Seeker (ID: PERSONA-patient_unknown_symptom-003-anxious-reassurance-seeker)

## Shared Claims
- Patients use online communities for information and support when causes/treatments are uncertain. (ID: CLAIM-20260224-002)
- Online themes include diagnostic concerns and anxiety. (ID: CLAIM-20260224-003)
- New/unfamiliar symptoms drive symptom checker use. (ID: CLAIM-20260224-004)
- Health anxiety predicts symptom checker use. (ID: CLAIM-20260224-007)
- Symptom checkers can unsettle anxious users. (ID: CLAIM-20260224-008)
